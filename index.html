Riffs On Beats

A few years ago, I challenged myself to come up with guitar riff, flesh it out in a full tune with drums and bass, and then record it. To make it easier to share and view - and to remember how to play it! - a video recording of my playing it was also made.

I did this every week for a year, posting these creations on @riffsonbeats on Instagram. After a brief break, I continued the series at a more sensible cadence, and still post new riffs every now and then.

Futro Guitars

I've played and owned many guitars most of my life and I've become pretty good at basic setup and maintanance. In recent years, I tried my hand at building guitars as well. When I come across a unique vintage part which had been orphaned but inspires me, I try give it a new life by preserving it's character and adding a few modern touches.

Futro #1 (2022)

This build started with a 1980s neck which I sanded and refinished. I leveled and polished the frets (the first time I'd done this) and ordered a specific body from the fine folks at Warmoth, in Tacoma. The unfinished body was stained with leather dye and finished with lacquer in my backyard.

Later that year, I delivered the guitar to my father in Belgrade.

Futro #0

This stratocaster clone was spec'd and assembled to match an exceptional second-hand neck and vintage Fender pickups. The USA-made swamp ash body was finished and aged by MJT in Missouri. I asselbmed and lightly aged the rest to match it's vibe.
